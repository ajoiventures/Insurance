This property has been uninsured since 2022 — closing that gap is the priority. You now have three options. American Integrity ($1,715, via GreatFlorida) is the cheapest by far and actually solves the Other-Structures underinsurance problem with a $48,000 limit — but its DP1 form excludes personal property entirely, a real coverage-breadth trade-off, not just a price difference. Geovera ($3,365) is a broader DP3 form but excludes water damage and is a non-admitted surplus carrier with non-refundable fees. Citizens ($4,190 + ~$500–700 liability) is pricier and slower to bind (30-day wait) but is the standard admitted market. Get LIG's full Citizens breakdown and confirm what American Integrity's DP1 form actually excludes before deciding — don't let this run past hurricane season prep without some coverage in place.

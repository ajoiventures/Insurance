Your home is currently insured, but that policy costs $4,660 a year and is coming up for renewal. We found a better option.

Our recommendation
CORE Insurance (through Rigetta Insurance)
$3,463 a year (about $289 a month)
  • Saves you about $1,197 a year compared to your current policy.
  • It's the only option we found that covers sinkhole damage — a real risk in this part of Florida — and has no limit on water damage claims (like a burst pipe).
  • Almost every other option is either less coverage for a similar price, or a lot more expensive.
If you'd rather save more money
There is a cheaper option — Mangrove Insurance, $2,375 a year (about $198/month) — but it covers a smaller rebuild value for the house, and doesn't include sinkhole protection unless you pay about $233 more per year for it.
Worth knowing
Before switching, the agent will need a fresh home inspection (a "4-point" and "wind mitigation" inspection) — this is normal and usually just a short visit from an inspector.
